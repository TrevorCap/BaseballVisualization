Welcome to the MLB Player Origins Website! Here you will find two interactive maps and a pie chart that help visualize where MLB Players come from. The first map shows all of the birth places of every MLB player since it was first established. The second map visualizes all of the places that MLB Players have passed away. Finally, the pie chart visualizes the make up of the league's globalism which can be edited to select specific ranges based on year.

The hope of this website is to allow the viewer to visually demonstrate both the concentration and the spread of baseball as a global sport over time. It will also demonstrate if there are locales which lend themselves well to the production of major league baseball players.
